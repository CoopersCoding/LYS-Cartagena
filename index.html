About The Event

Join us for our 2nd Annual Cartagena, Colombia Last Trip Single and immerse yourself in a renewing experience of the mind, body and soul with Jackie and David Dorman in this captivating retreat. Cartagena, Colombia is a one-of-a-kind destination and is often celebrated as one of the top romantic cities in South America, and a favored wedding destination for international celebrities.

Cartagena has always been a perfect backdrop for love and the city’s rich cultural legacy has earned it a position as a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Plus, its year-round tropical climate, exotic islands, local produce and welcoming people make for an unparalleled experience. Explore the reasons why a retreat in Cartagena de Indias is just what your soul’s been craving.

Please note: The Last Trip Single to Cartagena wouldn’t be a friendly trip for someone with physical limitations and is only available to women.

  • What is the expected weather?

    The weather in Cartagena in April sees average daytime temperatures of 31°C (88°F) and a drop to around 25°C (77°F) at night. This is slightly warmer than the January-March average range of 24°C (75°F) to 30°C (86°F). April is also usually more humid than the preceding months as the rain season starts to kick in.
